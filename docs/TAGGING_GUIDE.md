# ORAN Service Tagging Guide

> **For Organizations & Data Stewards**: How to accurately tag your services so people who need them can find them.

---

## Why Tagging Matters

ORAN helps people in crisis find services they qualify for. Accurate tagging is the bridge between your service and the person who needs it.

**Poor tagging → invisible service → unserved community members**

When someone searches "I need halal food near me, I use a wheelchair" — your halal food pantry with wheelchair access will only appear if you've tagged it correctly.

---

## The Tagging System

ORAN uses **structured tags** across multiple dimensions. Each dimension answers a different question a seeker might ask.

### Quick Reference Table

| Dimension | Question | Example Tags |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Delivery** | How do I get the service? | `in_person`, `virtual`, `mobile_outreach`, `home_delivery` |
| **Cost** | What does it cost? | `free`, `sliding_scale`, `medicaid`, `ebt_snap` |
| **Access** | How do I get in? | `walk_in`, `no_id_required`, `weekend_hours` |
| **Culture** | Who is it designed for? | `lgbtq_affirming`, `trauma_informed`, `spanish_speaking_staff` |
| **Population** | Who does it prioritize? | `veteran_family`, `dv_survivor`, `undocumented_friendly` |
| **Situation** | What crises can it handle? | `no_fixed_address`, `substance_use_active`, `fleeing_violence` |
| **Adaptations** | What disabilities/conditions? | `deaf`, `autism`, `diabetes` |
| **Dietary** | What food restrictions? | `halal`, `kosher`, `vegan`, `diabetic_friendly` |

## Step-by-Step Tagging Process

### Step 1: Start with Delivery Method

**Question**: *How does someone receive this service?*

Most services have 1-3 delivery methods. Be specific:

| If your service... | Tag with |
|-------------------|----------|
| Requires in-person visit | `in_person` |
| Offers video/phone appointments | `virtual` |
| Delivers to client's home | `home_delivery` |
| Has a mobile van/team | `mobile_outreach` |
| Operates at schools | `school_based` |
| Goes to encampments | `encampment_services` |

**Example**: A food pantry with drive-through and delivery options:
- ✅ `in_person`, `drive_through`, `delivery_available`

---

### Step 2: Define Cost & Payment

**Question**: *What does this cost and what payment do you accept?*

Be honest and complete. Seekers filter by what they can afford.

| If your service... | Tag with |
|-------------------|----------|
| Is completely free | `free` |
| Adjusts fees by income | `sliding_scale` |
| Takes Medicaid | `medicaid` |
| Takes Medicare | `medicare` |
| Takes EBT/SNAP | `ebt_snap` |
| Doesn't require insurance | `no_insurance_required` |
| Free for veterans | `free_for_veterans` |

**Example**: A sliding-scale clinic that takes Medicaid and uninsured patients:
- ✅ `sliding_scale`, `medicaid`, `no_insurance_required`

---

### Step 3: Specify Access Requirements

**Question**: *How does someone get in? What are the barriers?*

**Be honest about barriers.** People appreciate transparency over arriving and being turned away.

| If your service... | Tag with |
|-------------------|----------|
| Accepts walk-ins | `walk_in` |
| Requires appointments | `appointment_required` |
| Needs a referral | `referral_required` |
| Doesn't need ID | `no_id_required` |
| Doesn't need SSN | `no_ssn_required` |
| Is open weekends | `weekend_hours` |
| Is open evenings | `evening_hours` |
| Has same-day availability | `same_day` |
| Provides childcare | `childcare_available` |
| Helps with paperwork | `form_assistance` |
| Provides transportation | `transportation_provided` |

**Example**: A shelter that requires intake but no ID:
- ✅ `appointment_required`, `no_id_required`, `accepting_new_clients`

---

### Step 4: Cultural Competency & Affirmation

**Question**: *Who is this service designed for or especially welcoming to?*

Only claim what you've earned. Don't tag `lgbtq_affirming` without actual training and practice.

| If your service... | Tag with |
|-------------------|----------|
| Is LGBTQ+ affirming (trained staff) | `lgbtq_affirming` |
| Uses trauma-informed practices | `trauma_informed` |
| Is faith-based | `faith_based` |
| Is explicitly secular | `secular` |
| Has harm reduction approach | `harm_reduction` |
| Has staff who speak Spanish | `spanish_speaking_staff` |
| Uses peer support model | `peer_support` |
| Serves Indigenous communities | `tribal_native` |

**Example**: An LGBTQ+ youth drop-in center:
- ✅ `lgbtq_affirming`, `youth_focused`, `peer_support`, `trauma_informed`

---

### Step 5: Population Focus

**Question**: *Does this service prioritize or exclusively serve specific populations?*

Use these tags when you have **specific programming** for a population, not just general eligibility.

| If your service prioritizes... | Tag with |
|-------------------------------|----------|
| Veterans' families | `veteran_family` |
| Formerly incarcerated | `reentry` |
| Domestic violence survivors | `dv_survivor` |
| Foster youth | `foster_youth` |
| Refugees | `refugee` |
| Undocumented individuals | `undocumented_friendly` |
| Pregnant individuals | `pregnant` |
| Transition-age youth (16-24) | `transition_age_youth` |
| DACA recipients | `daca` |

**Example**: A job training program for formerly incarcerated women:
- ✅ `reentry`, `gender_specific_women`

---

### Step 6: Situational Barriers

**Question**: *What crisis situations or barriers does your service accommodate?*

These tags tell seekers: "You're welcome here even if you're dealing with X."

| If you serve people who... | Tag with |
|---------------------------|----------|
| Have no fixed address | `no_fixed_address` |
| Are actively fleeing violence | `fleeing_violence` |
| Are currently using substances | `substance_use_active` |
| Have no ID/documents | `no_documents` |
| Have no bank account | `no_bank_account` |
| Lack transportation | `transportation_barrier` |
| Have limited tech access | `digital_barrier` |
| Have criminal records | `criminal_record` |

**Example**: A harm reduction service:
- ✅ `substance_use_active`, `no_id_required`, `no_fixed_address`, `harm_reduction`

---

### Step 7: Service Adaptations (Disability/Health)

**Question**: *Does your SERVICE (not building) accommodate specific disabilities or health conditions?*

This is different from physical accessibility. A counseling service might be in an inaccessible building but still offer ASL interpreters.

| If your service accommodates... | Tag (adaptation_type: tag) |
|--------------------------------|----------------------------|
| Deaf/hard of hearing | `disability: deaf` |
| Blind/low vision | `disability: blind` |
| Autism spectrum | `disability: autism` |
| Cognitive disabilities | `disability: cognitive` |
| HIV/AIDS patients | `health_condition: hiv_aids` |
| Diabetes management | `health_condition: diabetes` |
| Infants (0-12 months) | `age_group: infant` |
| Seniors (65+) | `age_group: senior` |
| ESL learners | `learning: esl` |
| Low literacy | `learning: low_literacy` |

**Example**: A mental health clinic with ASL and autism accommodations:
- ✅ `disability: deaf`, `disability: autism`, `disability: mental_health`

---

### Step 8: Dietary Options (Food Services Only)

**Question**: *If you provide food, what dietary restrictions can you accommodate?*

| If you provide... | Tag with |
|------------------|----------|
| Halal food | `halal` |
| Kosher food | `kosher` |
| Vegan options | `vegan` |
| Gluten-free options | `gluten_free` |
| Diabetic-friendly | `diabetic_friendly` |
| Baby food/formula | `baby_food` |
| Pet food | `pet_food` |

Also specify **availability**:
- `always` — Always available
- `by_request` — Available with advance notice
- `limited` — When donated/in stock
- `seasonal` — Seasonal only

**Example**: A food pantry with halal by request and always available vegetarian:
- ✅ `halal (by_request)`, `vegetarian (always)`, `fresh_produce (always)`

---

### Step 9: Location Accessibility

**Question**: *What physical accessibility features does your BUILDING have?*

| If your location has... | Tag with |
|------------------------|----------|
| Wheelchair accessible | `wheelchair` |
| Accessible restroom | `accessible_restroom` |
| Accessible parking | `accessible_parking` |
| Elevator | `elevator` |
| Ground floor service | `ground_floor` |
| Service animals welcome | `service_animals_welcome` |
| Sensory-friendly space | `sensory_friendly` |

---

### Step 10: Transit & Parking

**Transit access** (array, select all that apply):
- `bus_stop_nearby`
- `subway_nearby`
- `paratransit_accessible`
- `bike_friendly`
- `walkable`

**Parking** (select one):
- `yes` — Free parking on-site
- `street_only` — Street parking only
- `paid` — Paid parking
- `no` — No parking

**Service**: Community Health Clinic — Mental Health Wing

**Description**: Outpatient mental health counseling serving San Jose. Specializes in trauma recovery and immigrant mental health. Sliding scale fees, accepts Medi-Cal.

- `in_person`, `virtual`, `phone`

- `immigrant`, `refugee`, `asylum_seeker`, `daca`, `undocumented_friendly`

**Situation:**
- `mental_health_crisis`, `fleeing_violence`, `language_barrier`

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Over-tagging
Don't tag what you don't actually provide. If you don't have halal food, don't tag it.

### ❌ Under-tagging
Don't assume people know. If you're open weekends, tag it explicitly.

### ❌ Confusing service vs. location accessibility
- **Service adaptation**: "Our counselors can use ASL" → `disability: deaf`
- **Location accessibility**: "Our building has a ramp" → `wheelchair`

### ❌ Tagging only aspirational features
Only tag what exists TODAY, not what you plan to add.

### ❌ Ignoring situational barriers
If you serve unhoused people, tag `no_fixed_address`. Don't make them guess.

## Questions?

If you're unsure how to tag a service, ask yourself:
> "If I were a person in crisis searching for exactly what this service provides, what would I type?"

Then tag for those signals.
