# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

ORAN is a civic-grade, safety-critical project. We are committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive collaboration environment for maintainers, contributors, and users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes, not people.
- Assume good intent while holding a high quality bar.
- Prioritize user safety, privacy, and factual accuracy in all discussions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Doxxing, threats, or publication of private/sensitive information.
- Deliberate misinformation about safety-critical behavior.
- Repeated disruption of issue, PR, or review processes.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

For severe or repeated violations, maintainers may suspend or ban participation in this repository.

## Reporting

- Security or privacy concerns: follow `SECURITY.md`.
- Conduct concerns: open a private maintainer communication channel when possible, or file an issue with minimal sensitive detail.

## Maintainer standards

Maintainers are expected to enforce this policy consistently, document major moderation actions, and prioritize de-escalation where possible.
